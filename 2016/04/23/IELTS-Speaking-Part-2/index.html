  • Describe a modern building.

    When it comes to a modern building, the Marina Bay Sands pop into my mind. It is ship like stucture give me an incredable impression. I was astonished by its amzing outlook at the firs glance it. You can easily spot it far away due to its unique appeareance. It is like a gaint ship traveling on the sky.
    The boat in the sky is actually a park, which is called SkyPark. On the platform, you can have a bird’s view of whole Singapore.
    As I know, it is built as integreted resorts which means you can enjoy a wide variety of services within this single building. Inside it, there are many world-class chefs cooked for you. And you can aslo enjoy your luxurious lifes by gambling in its gorgeous casino. Nodoubltly, as long as you are rich, this building can provide first class services to you. In my opinion, it already become a landmark of Singapore which I will introduce it to any friends or relatives who come to vist Singapore.
