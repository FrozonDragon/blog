    • 雅思7分奥秘

    8 分 要求

    表达流利，偶尔出现重复或自我纠正的情况；
    出现犹豫通常是基于思考内容，仅在少数情况下是寻找合适的语言
    连贯且恰当的展开话题
    词汇使用丰富，运用自如及灵活，表达意思准确
    熟练地使用非常见的词汇及习语，偶尔欠准确
    按需进行有效的改述；灵活地使用多种语法结构
    除极其偶然情况下出现的不当或简单的，非系统性错误外，大部分语句准确无误。
    使用多样的发音特点表达过程中灵活地使用多种发音特点。
    但偶尔出现偏差，表达过程中始终易于听者理解；母语的口音对听者理解的影响极小。

    It is not what you say, but how you say it.

    1. 说英语开始注重语音语调，“用力说”。
    2. 展示运用复杂句型的能力。
    3. 展示运用多种多样词汇的能力。
    4. 展示严谨思维，提高说话连贯度

    Pronunciation

    1. Correct
    2. Intonation (语音语调)
    3. Stress
    4. Emotion

    Grammer Fluency & Coherence

    Bad: I like basketball very much. It is interesting. I play it everyday

    Well, I would probably say that I am the person who is really into ….
    as I believe it is …… It would be so nice if I could have the opportunity to … every single day. I do …. with a bunch of friencds on a regular basis, which is a fantastic way to relax myself.

    Well, at the beginning I thought that … is just something that I found quite tough and complicated. Gradually, I found it is actually not as difficult as I thought. Luckily, I was able to do it after I put some efforts on it.

    You know, I am an accounting student, therefore, I am always under great pressure because of the exams I have. Even I only have classes 10 hours a week, I still don’t have much free time since I ned to spend my evening on my home work. So, as long as I have free time.

    I woud … (do sth) as it is a good way to relax myself.

    I have always wanted to … since I was very little. I was going to But I just did not have the time. My life (….) could have been much better if …. I could have done ….. if I had the chance to ….
