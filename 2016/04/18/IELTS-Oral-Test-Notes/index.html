Day 1 True Lies

Q: Do I have to sound positive or optimistic? Do I have to give the examiner completely original or creative answers?
A: No.

One important point to emphasize here is that the marking system does NOT include references to the following points:

  • Interesting content
  • Amusing or funny answers
  • Body Language
  • The truth
  • Apprearance or dress

Samples with grade 7

Do you enjoy looking for gifts for people?

Yes, I do because selecting the right gifts can be really fun. The choices may seem endless, but I always try to consider my friends’ interests and personality. I can’t really afford expensive gifts. But I believe it’s the thought that counts.
My Answer: No, selecting gifts is a tedious work. Anytime when I want to buy somethings as gifts to my relatives or my friends, I need consider many aspects, such as quality, price, color and their tasties. You know, it is really hard to buy something they exactly want as presents. Hence, most of time, I would prefer to ask before I buy anything.

Do you sometimes prefer to send a text message instead of telephoning?

Yes. I prefer to text when the message I want to send isn’t urgent, because that allows me more time to think things through and allows the receiver more time to think about how to respond. Texting is also much cheaper than calling.
My Answer: Yes, personally I prefer to sending text message instead of calling the person directly. Firstly, I can think twice of what I want to express before I sending out the messages in case that some inappropriate ways of expressions or contents may upset the listeners. Secondly, I consider an unscheduled telephoning as a unwelcomed and disturbing event for the receiver unless the one is your close friend or your family memeber. Lastly, nowadays, the texting messages can also embedded with some animated pictures which make the messages less boring, and easy to understand my feeling or my mood when I typing.

Do you enjoy the advertisements on television?

I would say I enjoy some of them because they are creative and witty. But it’s true most ads just spoil the fun of watching TV, and the information they bring us tends to be misleading.
My Answer: Ok, it really depends on the quality of Ads. I am fond of ads which are creative or amusing, expecially those ads with pop music. And I also like those ads acted by my idol without any reasons. The most disgusted Ads is the one just keeps repeating the same slogan all the time.

Do you like making other people laugh?

Sure, I know lots of jokes and really enjoy sharing them with friends of mine. It seems like I just happen to have a unique sense of humor. I tend to believe humor is essential to a good mood at work and at home as well.
My answer: Yes, No doubtly, I like making other people laugh although I am good at it. I admire those people who can amuse others with appropreviate stories. But we need be care of not upset other people by teasing someone’s shortcomings.

What’s your favourite suject at school?

Humm, I guess it’s PE, which stands for physical education. Sometimes we call it the gym class. PE not just keeps us physically fit, it gets us more focused on academic subjects as well.

What’s your favourite season?

Well, I would say summer. Actualy, the summer in Beijing is really hot, scorching! But in summger, my friends and I can just hang out together in places like malls or Starbucks. The summer vacation is long, much longer than the spring break. And the coolest thing about summer is we can just wear casual clothes like tees and shorts.

Some phrases

  1. To keep fit, I work out (=exercise) in the gym for an hour every day.
  2. I’m studying hard because I don’t want to let my parents down (=disappoint sb.)
  3. DIY stands for Do-It-Yourself.
  4. The work piles up (becomes more) but I will keep at it.
  5. We played games to liven up the party.
  6. I need to polish up (imporve) my english before I go to Australia.
  7. Steven Chow’s comedies never fail to crack me up. (make me laugh)
  8. It was handed down from my grandmother.
  9. Reading helps me wind down. (=relax)
  10. These old photos bring back memories of my childhood.

What can I possibly do if I can’t fully understand a question?

  1. Could you please rephrase the question?
  2. Well, my best guess would be …

Am I supposed to constantly update my knowledge about current events for the speaking test?
No. IELTS only cares for those trends.

Day 2: The untold story

Part 1 –> Chat

If possible, 3 sentences per question.

Part 2 –> Description

  1. 描述时一定会有适当的思考和停顿
  2. 描述需要有一定的规划，需要有秩序
  3. 句式会有一些变化

  1. 会用到很多表示逻辑关系的连接词。
  2. 会要求考生的答案比Part 1 和Part 2 正式一些。
  3. The good news is there is no need for you to make it as formal as a job interview.

  • Do you wish to own a boat? Why?

    No, I won’t be able to find a place to store it. And Maintenance of a boat can be a lot of work.

  • Do you think it’s impolite to use mobile phones in public places?

    Well, it’s impolite to use mobile phones in a public place like a cinema, library or classroom where people are supposed to be quiet. But I guess it’s kind of okay to use mobile phones in public palces like shopping malls where it’s always noisy anyway, if we don’t mind others hearing our phone conversation.

  • What are some of the good ways that can help people sleep better?

    There’re a number of good ways to improve sleep. For example, keeping a regular sleep schedule definitely helps to improve sleep. Also, doing some light exercise before bedtime makes the body feel that it needs a good night’s rest. Some other ways include doing some leisure reading or taking a hot shower. Basically, anything that helps us relax right before bedtime can help us sleep better.
    My answer: Actually, I was confronted (tormented /) with (got the problem of) sleeplessness before. I did a simple search on how to improve sleep. There are several ways to enhance the quality of sleep, including drinking a hot milk, listening to light music, reading a leisure book and do lots of body exercises to make yourself physically tired. It turns out that only listening to light music works for me. I found the secret to sleep well is to clear your mind or free your brain of any thinkings.

  • Do you think people should have different bags for different occasions? Why?

    Yes, I do. Bags should go well with the clothes we wear, and always suit the occasion. A mismatched bag may give people a wrong impression about us, or make us look out of place. For example, it looks kind of weird to carried a backpack while wearing formal office clothing.

  • Do you think it’s important to learn a musical instrument? Why?

    Yes, I do because the ability to play a musical instrument is very important. It helps us gain a better understanding of different types of music and different cultures. Learning a musical instrument also helps us to develop self-discipline because it’s challenging and takes a lot of time and effort.

  • Do you like to send postcards to friends? Why?

    When I travel, I like to send postcards to my friends and family from my travel destinations. That’s a great way to stay in contact with my friends and family and show them what I’ve seen. Any they’re always happy to receive postcards from me and know I’m safe and enjoying my trip.
